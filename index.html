Premise

A hidden force known as Life Energy flows through every human being. Only a rare few awaken it.

Those who do become Nephilims — warriors capable of manifesting Spades, abilities born from instinct, emotion, and identity.

Nephilims aren’t chosen by gods. They’re shaped by who they are.

Main Cast

Kai Shinsui

He's reckless yet calm. he's stubborn. he's instinctive in battle. he hates being controlled.

Spade: Copycat – A Spade that amplifies speed, reflexes, combat instinct and can copy other Spades.

Weapon: Breaker Katana (Legendary)

Ryo Shinsui

He's hot-headed. he's proud. he thrives in chaos and lives for the thrill of battle.

Spade: Steelbound – Converts Resonance into condensed energy that can pierce or shield anything.

Core Weapon: Breaker Gloves (Legendary)

Mira Vale

She's confident. she's playful. she's intelligent. she treats combat like a dance.

Spade: Pulse – Precision timing, control, and devastating force.

Ash Kuroda

He's disciplined. he's strategic. The “perfect” Nephilim. he believes control outweighs power.

Spade: Ignition – Explosive bursts of Resonance for high-speed impact attacks.

  • Life Energy fuels all Nephilim abilities.
  • Strong emotions increase output.
  • Poor control destabilizes techniques.
  • Depletion causes temporary paralysis.
